About Us

We a professional higher institution that admit students following GCE “A” level in various disciplines health disciplines. Our programs are fashioned and tailored to meet the increasing demands of health care in our communities. Our various disciplines include; Nursing, Medical Laboratory Science, Midwifery, Pharmacy Technology and Physiotherapy. Upon graduation our graduates earn a Higher National Diploma (HND) in their various disciplines. Professional education is the song on every lips of a higher institution. The wide margin however, that separates this chorus of professional education and what we actually find in the field is alarming. Private Higher education went operational in Cameroon in 2002 with Bamenda University of Science and Technology (BUST) and Universite de Montain, being the first to be approved. The real reason was to enable the private sector come in with a more professional approach. We have seen a tremendous increase in the number of private higher institutions in the country. As a product of one of these institutions in 2009 and subsequently also a trainer since 2011, the Promoter of LACOHEST has witnessed a gradual but steady decline in the quality of most graduates from these schools. This is one of the reasons for high unemployment among these graduates since they can’t measure up with basic competencies needed after graduation. A large number of them still depend on jobs from the public service as you see them change one profession to another just for the search of the so call “matricule number”. It is therefore the goal of LACOHEST to pull stake holders back to the drawing board as we reset the pace for professional education in Cameroon.

    • Our Mission

    We emphasise on a holistic approach of training such that the “head” the “hands” and the “heart” are all fully involved. This is possible because our principles respect the standards for class size and the quality of lecturers we recruit. We lay much emphasis on learning through practice (and not just theorizing) from class room settings, through our practical/demonstration units to our partner industries which are all well-equipped. Our lecturers purposefully engage students in direct experience and focused reflection in order to increase knowledge, develop skills, clarify values and develop their capacity to contribute to their communities. Irrespective of the area of study, all our students are obliged to undertake computer and must earn good grades with sufficient knowledge in Microsoft package upon graduation. Leadership, entrepreneurial and managerial courses are also inextricably incorporated with our programs. We are “Doers” and not “Talkers”

    Objectives

    To improve on the quality of higher education by providing experiential training to our students with a target of about 2000 students trained each year from the 5th year of our existence.Ensuring that our graduates maintain an employment rate of not less than 90% To make Higher education more accessible and affordable to the underprivileged by providing a scholarship scheme that caters for such needs.To provide employment to at least 300 professionals, most of them youths before the end of the 5th year.By the end of the 5th year, LOCOHEST will also be involved in the construction of other social facilities like hospitals and bore holes.

    Vision

    Our vision is to limit the rate of unemployment among Cameroonian youths by training leaders and job creators through the provision of experiential-styled education as well as competent oriented approach training. Upon graduation all our graduates possess sufficient skills not just to compete but to lead nationally and internationally in the job market. Such skills will enable them not just to seek jobs but to create meaningful sustainable jobs to the thousands of youths who are unemployed.
